Wildfires in the United States (2000-2018)

The map displayed above shows wildfire incidents that have occurred in the United States between 2000 and 2018. To use the map, enter a fire year in the search prompt to display all the wildfires that have occurred within that year. Then search for the wildfire incident name in the search bar or select a fire incident to view its information. The information includes the state where the fire occurred, the year the fire occurred, how many acres burned, and the responsible land agency. The data was obtained from the National Interagency Fire Center.

Lesson 7 Reflection

For this assignment, I created a web application focused on wildfires in the United States as they have a significant impact on botanical species, making wildfires a crucial concern for me as a botanist. The data includes infamous wildfires in Nevada such as the Fork fire and Goodsprings fire, both of which required massive restoration efforts that included seeding and outplanting. In the previous assignment, I used visual variables to display data, so for this lesson, I opted to display data using the Class Break Renderer in order to practice with different renderer code. I also utilized Color Brewer to obtain logical color values that represented the wildfires burned by size (in acres).

I found this lesson to be much more straightforward due to having established better understanding and knowledge from previous lessons. The module lessons also provided helpful guidance on how to accomplish the required tasks such as creating the search widget and performing a query using definition expressions. However, I encountered difficulties understanding the concepts presented in Lesson 7.2.2, "Getting Feature Counts," and Lesson 7.2.3, "Promises," particularly in regards to understanding the methods used in each example. In order to gain a better understanding, I would have to revisit these concepts multiple times and view additional examples until I feel more confident. In addition, I encountered a challenge with my map failing to load, but later realized that the sequential order of my code was incorrect and was then able to fix the issue.
